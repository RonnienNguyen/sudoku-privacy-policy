# Privacy Policy for Sudoku Brain

**Last updated: January 30, 2026**

Sudoku Brain ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect

Sudoku Brain does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

The app may collect non-personal information automatically, such as:

- Device type
- Android version
- App usage data (for performance and crash analysis)

This data is used solely to improve app stability and user experience.

## Children's Privacy

Sudoku Brain does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.
If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will remove it immediately.
